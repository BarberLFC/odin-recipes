Cheese Burger

How good does this look?

I love Cheese Burgers so much, but only ever really get it on special occassions.

I wish I could eat it every day tbh :)

Ingredients

  • Beef Burger
  • Bread bun
  • Choice of sauce
  • Pickles/Onions
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Burger
  2. Add the sauce to you bread bun
  3. Add the Burger to the bun
  4. Top with cheese and pickle/onions
