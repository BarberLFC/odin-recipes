Lasagna

How good does this look?

I love Lasagna so much, but only ever really get it on special occassions.

I wish I could eat it every day tbh :)

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Red sauce
  • White sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta sheets

Steps

  1. Brown the mince in a frying pan
  2. Mix in the red sauce
  3. Add the sauce mix as a layer to a dish
  4. Add a layer of white sauce
  5. Add a layer of pasta sheets
  6. Repeat these steps until you have used all the ingredients
  7. Add a final layer of chees
  8. Bake in the oven
